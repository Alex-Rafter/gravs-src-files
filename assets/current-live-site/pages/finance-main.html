Financial Disclosure Gravell's Limited is Fully Authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority (307976), as an Authorised Credit Broker and not a Lender. Gravell's Limited will introduce consumers to a select number of finance providers and may as a result receive from the finance provider a commission for Introduction. Gravell's Limited will typically receive a fixed commission calculated by reference to the vehicle model or amount a consumer borrows, but this does not affect the interest charged on the Agreement, all of which are set by the Lender. Gravells Limited do not have any discretion to negotiate or adjust the interest rate or APR charged by the Lender. Gravells Limited will upon request from a consumer, disclose any commission received relating directly to a consumer finance transaction.
