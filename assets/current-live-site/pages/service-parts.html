Service and Parts

We provide a complete after sale service including vehicle Service, repair, parts supply and have a Renault and KIA approved body-shop for vehicle repair. Our friendly staff are always happy to help, they take time to understand your requirements and do their best to exceed them!  Our technicians are professionally trained by our manufacturers and have the latest equipment to diagnose any issues. We offer a quick and efficient service with as little inconvenience to our customers as possible.  In most cases, we are able to supply a courtesy car at short notice so that we can keep you mobile. Please give us a call to discuss your needs or use the service & MOT booking-in form below.
