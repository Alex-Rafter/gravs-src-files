About Us

Gravells, a name synonymous with quality and service excellence, was founded in the picturesque town of Kidwelly in 1932 by Tom Gravell MBE. On March 1 1954, Gravells was awarded the Renault agency, making it the oldest Renault dealership in the United Kingdom.

In 2007, Gravells added Kia in Kidwelly, and now also has Kia garages in Narberth, Swansea, Bridgend, Abergavenny and Hereford.

In addition to Renault and Kia, we offer the award-winning Dacia and SEAT ranges in Kidwelly. We currently sell over 2,500 new cars and vans annually, as well as over 5,000 used vehicles. We usually stock over 600 used cars across the group.

Our development has allowed us to give back to the community in southwest Wales. Currently, we are proud sponsors of the Scarlets as well as numerous local choirs, sporting and charity organisations.

We are also immensely proud of our ten year partnership with The Urdd, the Welsh Language Association, who amongst other valuable humanitarian activities, give the young people of Wales the chance to live vibrant lives through the medium of Welsh, learning at the same time to respect each other and people around the world. At Gravells, we operate a bilingual language policy, placing equal emphasis on Welsh and English for your convenience.

Despite the growth in business over the years, Gravells continues to pride itself on a friendly and personal service with the hope of exceeding your expectations. Gravells can cater for all your motoring needs; we offer a full aftersales service with factory trained technicians, a full service body shop for accident repairs, fully stocked parts and servicing areas, as well as a van centre that can cater for all your commercial vehicle requirements.

Thanks to our loyal customers, Gravells continues to thrive as a top performer amongst motor traders. Reflecting this success, Gravells continue to win manufacturer and community awards for their excellence in customer service and support to the local and wider Welsh community.


If you are looking for a quality car and value for money, Gravells is the place to come - we look forward to meeting you!
